The Best Movies according to Ian

My top 3 movies of all time

The Guardians of the galaxy

The reason why I absolutely love this film is because it is based on a group of people, that works to reflect a dysfunctional family, with an amazing soundtrack that will be unforgettable, James Gunn absolutely smashed it out of the park with this movie

Twisters

Tornados, lots of them, country music and beer, what else could you want from a film

Good Will Hunting

This is more than a movie, it gives beautiful life lessons, Matt Damon and Robin Williams are a perfect duo. I would recommend this just for the one line 'I am going to see about a girl'
